have performed an evaluation under the supervision and with the participation

procedures, as defined in Rule 13a-15e under the Securities Exchange Act of
1934 the Exchange Act. Based on that
evaluation, our management, including our CEO and CFO, concluded that our
disclosure controls and procedures were effective as of January3, 2004 to
ensure that information required to be disclosed by us in the reports filed or
